Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

No, you can book directly with our specialists without a referral. However, some insurance plans may require one.

Can I book tests online?

Yes, diagnostic tests and imaging services can be scheduled online through our patient portal or by calling our department directly.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or previous test results.
